Overview: Understanding Philippine GDP

Gross Domestic Product (GDP) is the total monetary value of all goods and services produced within a country‚Äôs borders over a specific time period. It is one of the most widely used indicators of a country's economic health and performance.

This dashboard presents an interactive analysis of the Philippine GDP from 2018 to 2023, broken down by Province and Highly Urbanized Cities (HUCs), and classified by industry such as Agriculture, Manufacturing, and Services.

Understanding GDP by region and industry helps us:

  • Identify economic strengths and weaknesses across the country
  • Track regional and industrial growth patterns over time
  • Support policy planning and investment decisions
  • Visualize the contribution of each province to national development
